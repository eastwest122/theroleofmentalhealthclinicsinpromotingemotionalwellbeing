Frequently Asked Questions

Q1: What is the difference between therapy and counseling?

A1: Therapy (or psychotherapy) typically involves deeper, long-term emotional work, exploring underlying patterns, trauma, or longstanding psychological dynamics. Counseling is often more short-term and goal-oriented, focusing on pragmatic issues, life transitions, or specific challenges. At New U Therapy Center & Family Services, we provide both — counseling for immediate support and therapy for deeper, sustained change.

Q2: Can I see a psychiatrist online through a mental health clinic?

A2: Yes. With telehealth advancements, many mental health clinics now offer telehealth psychiatrist services or online psychiatrist care. You can receive psychiatric evaluation, medication management, and follow-ups virtually from a licensed professional, often with the same standard of care as in-person visits. At our clinic, we offer both physical and virtual psychiatric care options to best suit your needs.
